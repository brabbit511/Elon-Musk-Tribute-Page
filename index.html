Elon Musk


A simple tribute page to Elon Musk, who is well-known for his passion in making the electric car a viable alternative to gasoline vehicles. Here are some of his other accomplishments:

  • 1995 - Started Zip2, a web software company, with his brother Kimbal. Zip2 developed and marketed an Internet "city guide" for newspapers.
  • 1999 - His online payment company, X.com, merged with Confinity, which had a money transfer service called PayPal. PayPal was acquired by eBay in 2002 for $1.5 billion (US).
  • 2002 - Founded Space Exploration Technologies, or better known as SpaceX. Musk is CEO and Chief Technology Officer (CTO). SpaceX develops and manufactures space launch vehicles with an empahsis on improving rocket technology.
  • 2006 - Provided the initial concept and capital for SolarCity, which was co-founded by his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive. SolarCity is now the second largest provider of solar power systems in the US.
  • 2006 - SpaceX was awarded a contract from NASA to further develop and test the SpaceX Falcon 9 launch vehicle and Dragon spacecraft in order to transport cargo to the International Space Station.
  • 2008 - NASA awarded SpaceX a $1.6 billion launch contract for 12 flights of the Falcon 9 rocket and Dragon spacecraft to the Space Station. SpaceX is currently the largest private producer of rocket motors in the world.
  • 2008 - Musk assumes CEO and product architect role of Tesla Motors. Tesla builds its first electric car, the Tesla Roadster, which sells about 2,500 vehicles in 31 countries.
  • 2012 - The Tesla Model S began delivery.
  • 2014 - Tesla Motors announced that its technology patents are allowed to be used by anyone in good faith, to entice auto manufacturers to increase development of electric cars.
  • 2016 - Tesla Motors announces that it will begin accepting preorders for the Tesla Model 3. The Model 3 will be the first Tesla vehicle priced for affordability, beginning at $35,000 US.

"When something is important enough, you do it no matter the odds."

Elon Musk
